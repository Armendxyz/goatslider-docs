# Future

We are working hard to bring you the best experience with our Sliders, and we are dedicated to that mission and we are taking the necessary steps to do so. In the near future, we plan to address and improve upon key areas we believe will benefit the community.

- New Premium and Free Templates
- Slide Indicators, Clickable Slides that change the slide
- Allow the default slide animations on click to happen
- More transition effects on Slider Type, such as cubic, zoom, and more

And more features that the community will request for their needs. So don't hesitate to reach out to us, share your ideas, and contribute to the community. We reward good ideas with premium slider templates sometimes.
